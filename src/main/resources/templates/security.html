Safe and Secure Shopping on Mymart

Is making online payment secure on Mymart?

Yes, making the online payment is secure on Mymart.

Does Mymart store my credit/debit card information?

No. Mymart only stores the last 4 digits of your card number for the purpose of card identification.

What credit/debit cards are accepted on Mymart?

We accept VISA, MasterCard, Maestro, Rupay, American Express, Diner's Club, and Discover credit/debit cards.

Do you accept payment made by credit/debit cards issued in other countries?

Yes! We accept VISA, MasterCard, Maestro, American Express credit/debit cards issued by banks in India and in several other countries.

What other payment options are available on Mymart?

Apart from Credit and Debit Cards, we accept payments via Internet Banking, Cash on Delivery, Equated Monthly Installments (EMI), E-Gift Vouchers, Mymart Pay Later, UPI, Wallet, and Paytm Postpaid.
