Grievance Redressal Mechanism

MyMart is duty-bound to provide fair treatment to our consumers and address their grievances effectively.

What does "Grievance" mean?

Grievance means any issue related to the product/service availed by the consumer from the MyMart Platform, for which the consumer seeks resolution.

Journey:

  1. Click on the 'Contact Us' or 'Help Centre' tab.
  2. It will open to MyMart Help Center | 24x7 Customer Care Support.
  3. Choose from Type of Issue / Help Topics.
  4. Submit your query.

Our ‘Grievance Redressal Mechanism’ is as follows:

  • Upon receipt of a Consumer Grievance, acknowledgment will be sent within 48 hours through email, phone call, or SMS.
  • The Consumer will receive a system-generated "Unique ID" to track the grievance status.
  • The Consumer Care and Grievance Officer will make best endeavors to resolve the grievance expeditiously within the prescribed timeline.
  • A Grievance will be considered closed and disposed-off in certain instances, such as when solutions are offered to the consumer.
